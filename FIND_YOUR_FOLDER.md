# 📁 Where Is My Project Folder?

You have all the files (I can see them!), but you need to find **where they are saved on your computer**.

---

## 🔍 How to Find Your Project Folder

### On Windows:
1. **Press the Windows key** (bottom left of keyboard)
2. **Type**: `image-to-prompt` or `App.tsx`
3. **Look for a folder** in the search results
4. **Click on it** to open

### On Mac:
1. **Press Cmd + Space** (opens Spotlight search)
2. **Type**: `image-to-prompt` or `App.tsx`
3. **Look for a folder** in the results
4. **Click on it** to open

### Alternative Way:
1. **Open your file explorer** (Windows) or **Finder** (Mac)
2. **Look in common places**:
   - Desktop
   - Downloads folder
   - Documents folder
   - A folder you created

### Option 1: Re-download
If you can't find where you saved it:
1. **Download the project files again**
2. **Save them to your Desktop** (easy to find)
3. **Remember where you put them!**

### Option 2: Create New Folder
1. **Create a new folder** on your Desktop
2. **Name it**: `my-image-app`
3. **Put all your files there**
4. **Now you know where it is!**
